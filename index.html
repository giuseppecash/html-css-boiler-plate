WHY CRAFT?

Fast, free, and easy to learn.

Built with Kotlin and open source,
Craft is the most efficient way to
create complex mods for Forge,
modpacks and custom clients.

Craft also solves most of the issues
that Java creates. No more janky
updating, slow compiling, or lack of
security.
